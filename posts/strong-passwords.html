Strong Passwords

Password's are one of the most important aspects of online security. There are several things you can do to make your password a strong one:

  • Have a length of at least 8 characters.
  • Use upper and lowercase letters
  • Have numbers and special characters such as: !@#%.
  • Never use an entire word
  • Never use the same password twice

If you follow these guidelines your passwords will be much stronger and will protect your personal information much better.
